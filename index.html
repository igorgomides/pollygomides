Our Core Philosophy

Designed for Action, Built for Results

Online language learning shouldn't be passive. We design cohorts that encourage constant peer collaboration, tailored support, and long-term skill retention.

Lifetime Video Access

All live online sessions are captured and systematically uploaded to Google Classroom within 24 hours. Missing a class will never mean missing out on key learning moments.

Exclusive 5-7 Cohorts

With size limitations of just 5 to 7 students per group, you'll benefit from individualized attention, detailed speaking opportunities, and a supportive, non-intimidating space.

Structured Weekly Cycle

No endless lectures. Each learning module consists of the live class link, a 1-page condensed "Cheat Sheet" for quick reference, and a practical "Action Step" task.

Your Mentor

Hi, I’m Polly Gomides

My passion is helping people reach their objectives as they strive to reach their goals.

Originally from Brazil, I grew up in Dallas, Texas and learned English as a child. My love for languages and cultures can be attributed to growing up with the awareness of the different cultures I came into contact with. I believe this is probably what inspired me to pursue a career in teaching English as a Second Language, which I have happily pursued for over 20 years.

With over 20 years of experience in English as a Second Language (ESL) education, I have dedicated my career to helping students bridge the gap between their current skills and the fluency they need to thrive. I understand that learning a language is more than just memorizing grammar; it is about building the confidence to communicate effectively in a new culture.

I know the insecurities and doubts that come with learning a new language because I have lived that journey myself. My mission is to provide an encouraging environment where you feel empowered to overcome linguistic barriers. Whether you are aiming for professional growth or personal fulfillment, I am here to ensure you have the tools and support to live out your dreams with total linguistic independence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Don't worry! All live classes are fully recorded and posted to our secure Google Classroom portal within 24 hours. You will also get the weekly Cheat Sheet and the assigned Action Step task to keep your progress on track.
Language acquisition requires active output. Large classrooms make it easy to stay passive. By capping our micro-cohorts at a maximum of 7 students, everyone is guaranteed ample speaking time, personal attention, and real-time custom feedback.
For our semester-long courses, you can pay a single full payment of $660 or choose an installment plan of 7 monthly payments of $94.28. For the Teacher Language Booster (Summer Intensive), it is $150 total, with options of 7 installments of $21.43 or 2 installments of $75.
A Cheat Sheet is a 1-page PDF summaries of vocabulary, idiomatic expressions, and syntax structures covered in class. An Action Step is a practical homework assignment—like submitting a short audio recording or a paragraph—so I can assess and offer personal feedback.
